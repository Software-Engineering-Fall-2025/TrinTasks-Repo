Welcome to TrinTasks!

Your calendar has been loaded successfully. Let's take a quick tour of all the features to help you stay organized.

Week Calendar

Click any day in the calendar to see assignments due that day. Today is highlighted, and the selected day has a border.

Week

Week View

Click the Week button in the header to see all assignments for the entire week grouped by day.

⋮

Assignment Actions

Click the three dots on any assignment to access actions: mark complete, mark in-progress, or set a reminder.

Reminders

Set reminders for individual assignments to get notified 1, 2, 4, or 24 hours before they're due.

Drag to Reorder

Drag and drop assignments to reorder them by priority. Your custom order is saved automatically.

Major Assignments

Enable the Major Assignments sidebar in Settings to see upcoming tests, quizzes, essays, and projects at a glance on the right side of the screen.

Settings

Settings

Click Settings to change themes, customize colors for each class, enable the major assignments sidebar, and configure reminders.
